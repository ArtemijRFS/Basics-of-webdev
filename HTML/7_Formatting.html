Formating elements - displays special types of texts

        Bold text - <b>
        Important text - <strong>
        Italic text - <i>
        Emphasised text - <b>
        Marked text - <mark>
        Small text - <small>
        Deleted text - <del>
        Inserted text - <ins>
        Subscript text - <sub>
        Superscript text - <sup>
